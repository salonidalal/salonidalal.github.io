EDUNET: A virtual classroom

Today in the world of digital things, traditional classes are being replaced by the virtual classrooms or digital classrooms. A benefit to taking online courses is that they offer flexibility to the student. This is a great option for those who already have time commitments with family and work. Online classes will mould with your scheduleâ€”and allow you to log into your online course at a time that works best for you, as opposed to having to attend a lecture at a specific time. One of the major drawbacks of online classrooms is that they lack the feel of traditional classrooms which most of the people are used to. Hence one of the major goals of our project was to make the feel of online class as realistic as possible.

KEY FEATURES

The project has features which aims at giving a feeling of live classroom environments like raise hand, whiteboard, sticky notes during live lectures. In addition to that, features that can be available only in virtual classrooms like taking a screenshots of the ongoing lectures that will be directly saved to the library have also been incorporated. A professor can easily upload videos of lectures which can be accessed by the students who missed the lectures later.
